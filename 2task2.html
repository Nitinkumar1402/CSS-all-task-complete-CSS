Briards

A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed Histry

The briard, which is believed to have originated in france, has been berd for conturies to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wouded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering, Charlemaggne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson and Lafayette all owned briards.
